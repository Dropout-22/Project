Sweet Corn Bread

Description

This corn bread goes well with a variety of dishes, including chili, stews, and soups. It's also a great side dish for a summer barbecue or picnic.

For the best results, try to obtain all ingredients listed as high quality or fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F(205°C).
  2. Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
  3. Combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Mix egg, sour cream, milk and butter in a small bowl.
  5. Fold egg mixture into the flour mixture until just moistened.
  6. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  7. Bake in preheated oven and insert a toothpick until the center comes out clean.
  8. After about 25 minutes, remove from the oven and serve it warm.
